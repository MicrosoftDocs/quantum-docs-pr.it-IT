### <a name="asserting-equality-of-quantum-operations"></a><span data-ttu-id="5f6bb-163">Asserzione di uguaglianza di operazioni Quantum</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5f6bb-163">Asserting Equality of Quantum Operations</span></span> ###
